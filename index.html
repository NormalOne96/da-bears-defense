HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE

Defend Soldier Field! Place Bears Legends as towers to stop NFL enemies. You have 20 lives — bosses steal 3-8 if they break through. Build & upgrade Soldier Field between runs to earn permanent perks!

TOWERS (16 BEARS LEGENDS)

  • Select a tower card from the bottom tray
  • Tap any gold-tinted open tile to place it
  • Each tower has 3 upgrade levels
  • Tap a placed tower to see Upgrade, Sell, Target options
  • Some enemies are stealthed — only certain towers can see them!

SOLDIER FIELD BUILDER

  • Kill enemies to earn Glory Points
  • Open the stadium from title or in-game button
  • Spend Glory to build stadium sections — each grants real gameplay perks
  • Perks persist across games!

BEARS LEGENDS

  • Sid Luckman — Multi-shot QB, hits 2-3 targets
  • Walter Payton — Ultra-fast, slows every hit
  • Mike Ditka — DA COACH, stuns enemies cold
  • Dick Butkus — AoE ground pound
  • Devin Hester — Chain lightning, 3-10 enemies
  • Brian Urlacher — Massive AoE field bomb
  • Richard Dent — Armor-piercing rounds
  • William Perry — Melee titan, AoE smash
  • Johnny Morris — Sniper, highest-HP target
  • Gale Sayers — Split shot on impact
  • Mike Singletary — Inspire aura, buffs nearby towers
  • Peanut Tillman — Peanut Punch! Steals coins on hit
  • Dan Hampton — Penetrating strikes, shreds armor
  • Lance Briggs — Rapid-fire tackle machine
  • Doug Atkins — Leaping strike, stuns + heavy damage
  • Gary Fencik — Reveals stealth, long-range interception

ENEMY TYPES

  • Normal — Standard enemies
  • Fast — High speed, low HP
  • Armored — Damage-resistant tanks
  • Stealth — Invisible! Only Fencik and upgraded towers can see them
  • Healer — Regenerates HP of nearby enemies
  • Splitter — Splits into 2 smaller enemies on death
  • Boss — Massive HP, devastating if they break through
